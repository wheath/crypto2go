Crypto2go is an initiative sparked by ideas: the ideas that more information leads to more agency, that cryptoassets bring value and power back into the hands of its holders, and that one good turn deserves another.

We aim to realize these ideas through facilitating cross-borders value transfer, presenting price-stable ways for people to hold their native currency on-chain, and through donations that engender goodwill amongst travelers.
